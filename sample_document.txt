*** GOVERNMENT OF INNOVATION & TECHNOLOGY DEPARTMENT ***
RFP Reference No.: GTD/PROC/2025/071
Issue Date: 05-Nov-2025
Subject: Procurement of IT Infrastructure Equipment (Switches, UPS, Racks, and Cables)

1. GENERAL OVERVIEW:
The department intends to upgrade its internal network and power systems across multiple locations.
Vendors are invited to submit bids for items described under the *Technical Requirement Summary*.
The proposal shall include warranty details, installation schedule, and post-support terms.

2. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENT SUMMARY

------------------------------------------------------------
ITEM: Layer 3 Core Switch
------------------------------------------------------------
Brief: High-performance core-level switch capable of handling enterprise traffic.
Main Specs:
• Model No - NetLink X5000
• Switching capacity : >= 320 Gbps
• Ports : (48x1G + 4x10G SFP+)
• Stacking support : Yes
• PoE : Not required
• Power Input - 230V AC ±10%
• Operating Temp Range: 0°C – 45°C
• Warranty Period → 5 years onsite

Additional Notes:
Should support SNMPv3, QoS prioritization, and VLAN segmentation up to 4096 IDs.

------------------------------------------------------------
ITEM: UPS System (Online)
------------------------------------------------------------
Description:
Rack-mounted double conversion UPS with pure sine wave output.
Specs:
  > Capacity 6 kVA, min runtime 40 mins at 80% load
  > Input: 160–280V AC range
  > Output voltage: 230V ±2%
  > Efficiency: >= 90%
  > Battery: Maintenance-free sealed lead-acid
  > Interface Port: USB / RS232 / Optional SNMP card
  > Audible Alarm: Yes
Warranty: Comprehensive 3 years
Certification: BIS / CE

------------------------------------------------------------
ITEM: Network Cabinet
------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Housing networking components and patch panels.
Key Specs:
1. Type – Floor Standing, 27U Height
2. Frame – Cold Rolled Steel (1.2mm thick)
3. Front Door – Glass with lock
4. Rear Door – Vented Metal
5. Fan Tray – 4 Fans
6. Color – Grey / Black options acceptable
7. Accessories: PDU, Mounting screws, cable tray
Warranty – 1 Year

------------------------------------------------------------
ITEM: CAT6 Cable (Roll)
------------------------------------------------------------
Length : 305 meters per roll
Compliance : EIA/TIA-568-C.2
Conductor : 23 AWG Solid Copper
Jacket : LSZH
Color : Blue (Preferred)
Test Report : Must be provided from certified lab

------------------------------------------------------------
3. EVALUATION CRITERIA
------------------------------------------------------------
- Technical compliance with specifications listed above.
- Delivery lead time (should not exceed 30 days).
- Vendor must have executed at least 3 similar projects in the past 2 years.
- Preference may be given to vendors offering extended warranty.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. COMMERCIAL TERMS
------------------------------------------------------------
- Payment: 80% after delivery, 20% after successful installation.
- Delivery: Department warehouse, Sector 27B, Chandigarh.
- Penalty: 1% per week delay, max 5%.
- Price: Inclusive of taxes, freight, and installation.
